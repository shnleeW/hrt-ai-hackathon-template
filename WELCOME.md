# 🎉 Welcome to the HRT Applied Research Hackathon!

Your workspace is ready. Let's get started.

## 👉 Step 1: Open Claude

Click the **Claude** (orange star) icon at the top right corner of the window.

If you don't see it yet, wait a few more seconds — the Claude extension is still installing.

## 👉 Step 2: Sign In

Sign in with your **Claude Team** account.

## 👉 Step 3: Build Your App

Tell Claude what you want to build! For example:

> "Build a dashboard that shows hotel occupancy by city."

## 💡 Useful Commands

Type these in the Claude Code chat:

- `/run` — Start the app and view it in your browser
- `/checkpoint` — Save your progress
- `/back-to-checkpoint` — Restore to your last checkpoint
- `/restart` — Reset everything to the start
- `/push` — Publish your work to GitHub for your portfolio

See `README.md` for the full list.

**Have fun building!** 🚀
